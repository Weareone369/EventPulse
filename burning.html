About Burning Man

Burning Man is an annual gathering that takes place at Black Rock City - a temporary city erected in the black Rock Desert in Nevada. It was first held in San Francisco on Baker Beach. The event started with the spontaneous burning of an 8-foot wooden effigy of a man as a small function. Organized and co-founded by Larry harvey and Jerry James in 1986. Since then, it has grown into a cultural phenomenon attracting tens of thousands of participants every year.
